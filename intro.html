Hey! I'm Michelle

2 years ago you couldn't have told me that I would be a software engineering student at a top coding bootcamp. I currently attend Hack Reactor's part time software engineering immersive where I learn the basics of software engineering and web-development.

I always thought of computer science and programming as subjects that were wayyyyy out of my depth. All of this, not knowing that I've actually been programming since high school. All that time I spent making my myspace page "poppin" by adding falling dollar signs and having Bokutachi no Yokue playing in the bacgkround, I was actually programming!

I decided to look into it further and ended up on this rollercoaster ride called my pivot into tech. After learning programming basics, there"s no other field I can see myself happier working. This is for me!

At the bottom of my blog I've added links to many of the amazing resources I've stumbled upon while learning to code. One thing I love about programming is that you are able to get started learning for free! I also love that there are so many guides out there to help you get started if you don't know where to begin.

Please follow me on my journey through tech and enjoy the progression of my projects as they go from basic to amazeeeeeeeinggg!
